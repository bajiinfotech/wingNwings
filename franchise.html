  • What Is The Size Of The Shop Required For Starting A Wing N Wings Unit Franchise?

    The franchisee has to find a suitable location, The minimum size of the shop should be 400 Sq.ft. however, the ideal space would be 500 Sq.ft or More

  • What is the Franchise Fee..?

    The unit franchise fee is RS. 700000+ GST. And 3% Royalty fee.

  • Are the Equipments Provided (Fridge,Freezer, etc) Branded ?

    Yes. All are branded products (Hair, Voltas etc.) and comes with ensured warranty.

  • What Are The Extra Expenses That Can Come Apart From The Franchise Fee Of 7 Lakhs?

    The shop should have tile flooring, kitchen partition, etc. Infrastructure development task ( painting, ply, partition, etc), Centre Furnishing / Improvement, etc.Electrical equipments & fittings & lighting, dining Arrangement : 20 dining ,interior Work ,FRONT COUNTER SETUP, The expenses for these will be extra. The franchise is responsible for any interior work, painting, and furniture. (The approximate cost will come around Rs. (4L/- to Rs.5L /-) Real estate charges like shop rent and security amount will also be extra.

  • Franchise Fee Refundable ?

    No.,Franchise Fee is Not Refundable.

  • How Long Is The Franchise Agreement? How Can It Be Renewed?

    The franchise agreement is valid for 3 years. At the end of the 3rd year, you can renew the agreement for another 3 years by paying 10% of the franchise fee..
